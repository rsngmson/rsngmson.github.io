About me

I am currently a fourth-year student at the University of Science, Vietnam National University. I serve as the research team leader at the Artificial Intelligence - Analytical Chemistry Laboratory under the supervision of Associate Professor Dr. Ta Thi Thao. Additionally, I am a student at the Natural Products Chemistry Laboratory, guided by Associate Professor Dr. Phan Minh Giang

Previously, in 2022 and 2023, I was a member of the Computational Chemistry Laboratory and the Biochemistry Laboratory at the Institute of Natural Products Chemistry, Vietnam Academy of Science and Technology.

My research interests are mainly:

  • AI for Science
  • Graph Machine Learning
  • Optimization
  • Chemometrics
